How do I play games? ▼
Go to the Games page and click on any game card. The game will open in a fullscreen player. Click the close button to return.
How do I use RedsssMessenger? ▼
Click "RedsssMessenger" in the navigation bar. You can create servers, join channels, and send direct messages to friends.
How do I add friends? ▼
Open RedsssMessenger and go to the Direct Messages view. Use the add friend button and type their username to send a friend request.
How do I change my profile picture? ▼
Click on your avatar in the top right corner to open your profile. Click the avatar in the dropdown to upload a new image.
