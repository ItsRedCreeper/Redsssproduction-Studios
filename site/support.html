Support

How do I play games? ▼
Go to the Games page and click on any game card. The game opens in a fullscreen player. Click Close to return.
How do I use RedsssMessenger? ▼
Click "RedsssMessenger" in the navigation bar. You can create servers, join channels, and send direct messages to friends.
How do I add friends? ▼
Open RedsssMessenger and go to the Direct Messages view. Use the Add Friend tab and type a username to send a friend request.
How do I change my profile picture? ▼
Click your avatar in the top right corner. In the dropdown, click the avatar image to upload a new picture.
I found a bug or have a suggestion ▼
Use the contact form below to send us a message and we'll look into it.
